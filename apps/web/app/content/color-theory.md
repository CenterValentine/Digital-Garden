## Hot and Cold Color Theory:

Red is hot (alerts)
Orangee and yellow are warm (warnings)
Green is cool (success)
Blue is cold (information)
Purple is neutral (neutral)
Gray is neutral (neutral)
Black is neutral (neutral)
White is neutral (neutral)

## Tints and Shade Paradigm:

Tints adds white to a color
Shades adds black to a color.

## Saturation, Hue, and Lightness paradigm:

Saturation is the intensity of a color. Desaturation...
Hue describes similarity or difference from a reference color.
Lightness is the amount of light to dark, dark to light, of a color.

## The Color Wheel Paradigm:

The color wheel is a circle of colors that are related to each other.
